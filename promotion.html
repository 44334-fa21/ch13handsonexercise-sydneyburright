How to promote the site

PPC

Pay per click. Pay a fixed price for every click that your ad gets in the search engine. The ultimate goal of the click is to convert that user in order to see a return on investment.

Retargeting

Retargeting ads place a cookie on each of the website's visitor's computers so they will start seeing ads to come back after they have already left your site.

Facebook ads

Facebook as well as many other social media platforms gain millions of users every day. Facebook ads can help target people from specific locations, demographics, etc. to bring in new traffic to your website.
